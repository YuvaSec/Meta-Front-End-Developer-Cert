
- A Conceptual framework used to understand and standardize how different computer systems communicate over a network.
![[50980_9775e912ec4ebe6cac252dabfb9219082522c6507df7eb74d39c92c28d2163f7.png]]

- It divides the complex process of network communication into seven distinct layers, each with specific functions
![[ackpb.png|760]]
- The model facilitates troubleshooting by allowing network professionals to pinpoint issues at specific layers.


***In essence,
the OSI model acts as a universal language and blueprint for network communication, making it easier to develop interoperable systems and diagnose networking issues across diverse platforms and technologies***
